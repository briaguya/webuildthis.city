###### Your plot plan must include:
* Property line with bearings and dimensions
* Required setbacks
* Existing / proposed building improvements
* Roof overhang
* Required off-street parking spaces (8.5ft x 17ft)
* Adjacent right-of-ways and surface material
* Existing / proposed sidewalks
* Existing / proposed driveways
* Driveway width at property line
* Driveway distance from storm sewer inlets
* Water Meter Location
* Electrical Service Location
* Natural gas meter location
* Trees greater than 19 inches in diameter
* Tree species and diameter
* Tree Critical root zone
* Property address
* Legal description
* North arrow
* Drawing scale
